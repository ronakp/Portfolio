About

From the dawn of my life, I have a very high tendency to fix things, to make things, especially, with computers. It would not be wrong to say that computers are my first love. So, I choose Computer Engineering in my Bachelor's study and become an Engineer. Even though I am an engineer from my heart, my brain survives on research and proofs. So, I choose Computer Science in my Master's study and become a Scientist.

As of my horizon of understanding and knowledge expanded, my invention-driven mind learns innovation capabilities. My heavy inclination towards entrepreneurial activities and startups also helped me in a development of innovation-driven mind.
